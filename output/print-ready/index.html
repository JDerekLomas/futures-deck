Understanding Bleed & Safe Zones

Bleed area — extends beyond cut, gets trimmed off
Cut line — where the card will be cut
Safe zone — keep text/important content here
Your design fills to the bleed edge. The printer cuts along the cut line
and rounds the corners. Content in the safe zone won't get trimmed.
